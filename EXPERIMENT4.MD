## 7. Display name, sal, HRA, DA, PF, and Total Salary

ðŸ“Œ  
- HRA = 15% of sal  
- DA = 10% of sal  
- PF = 5% of sal  
- Total Salary = (sal + hra + da) - pf  

```sql
SELECT 
    ename,
    sal,
    sal*0.15 AS hra,
    sal*0.10 AS da,
    sal*0.05 AS pf,
    (sal + (sal*0.15) + (sal*0.10) - (sal*0.05)) AS totalsal
FROM employee
ORDER BY totalsal DESC;
```
